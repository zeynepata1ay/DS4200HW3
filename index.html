Decoding Social Media Trends

Welcome! I'm Zeynep Atalay, and this website explores how trends go viral on social media. In today’s digital world, platforms like TikTok, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube shape global conversations and cultural moments. Some posts gain millions of views within hours, while others get buried in the endless sea of content. But what exactly makes certain trends explode while others fade into obscurity?

Social media virality isn’t random—it’s driven by algorithms, audience engagement, and timing. A TikTok dance might take off because influencers promote it, or a meme might spread rapidly due to its relatability. Hashtags, likes, shares, and video watch time all play crucial roles in determining what trends and what doesn’t.

I chose this topic because social media trends reflect how quickly information spreads and influences public opinion. As someone interested in data and technology, I find it fascinating to analyze how platforms determine what content gets amplified and why certain ideas resonate with millions of people.

This website takes a data-driven look at viral trends, analyzing key topics that have dominated social media in recent weeks. Whether it’s a new AI tool, a celebrity controversy, or a funny meme, understanding these trends helps us see how information spreads in the digital age.

Check out the Trends Page for a real-time visualization of what’s popular right now! From there, we can uncover patterns in viral content and explore what makes certain ideas take off.
